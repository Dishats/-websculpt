Welcome To ISTE Club

Welcome to ISTE , where students unite to foster creativity, collaboration, and personal growth. Our vibrant community is dedicated to providing a dynamic space for intellectual exploration, skill development, and meaningful connections .

VISION

Empowering students to thrive by cultivating a diverse and inclusive environment that nurtures innovation, critical thinking, and holistic development.

MISSION

To inspire a culture of lifelong learning and leadership through engaging activities, workshops, and events, fostering a sense of belonging and purpose among our members.

OBJECTIVES

Foster academic excellence through resources and mentorship. Enhance practical skills via hands-on workshops. Cultivate a diverse and supportive community. Facilitate career development through networking. Drive social impact through community service.

The Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE) is the leading National Professional non-profit making Society for the Technical Education System in our country with the motto of Career Development of Teachers and Personality Development of Students and overall development of our Technical Education System.

The Indian Journal of Technical Education is published by the Indian Society for Technical Education on quarterly basis. The contributors are expected to highlight various issues of Technical Education (incorporating disciplines of Engineering, Technology, Management, Architecture & Pharmacy, etc.,) along with meaningful suggestions for solution, refinement and innovations.
